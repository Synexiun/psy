# Help Center — Discipline OS

This is the full help content for Discipline OS, written in plain language. If you are using the app and want to understand what it is doing, why, or how, you will find it here.

**Locales:** Source content is in English. Validated translations into French, Arabic, and Persian are shipped at v1.0; each article carries a last-reviewed timestamp per locale.

**Reading level:** Target grade 9 or below (Flesch-Kincaid). If an article feels too clinical, it is a bug in the writing and we want to fix it.

## How this help is maintained

Every article is reviewed at least once a year by our Clinical Content Lead and by a native-speaker clinician for each translated locale. If you notice anything here that is unclear, wrong, or out of date, you can reach us through in-app support; this gets read and it changes the article.
