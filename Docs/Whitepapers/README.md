# Discipline OS — Methodology & Research Whitepapers

This folder holds the public-facing, research-grade documentation of Discipline OS. Every clinical claim made in product marketing, help content, or clinician-facing materials must trace back to a whitepaper in this set, and every whitepaper's claim must trace to a peer-reviewed source or a pre-registered internal study.

## Citation discipline

- All cited papers must be resolvable (DOI, PubMed ID, or published URL).
- Where we state a number (e.g. "sensitivity 0.88"), the cited paper must report that number.
- Where we describe a theoretical construct (e.g. "abstinence violation effect"), we cite its originating publication.
- Translations of psychometric instruments must cite the validation paper specific to that translation, not just the source instrument.

## Governance

- Clinical Content Lead owns this folder.
- Every change to Methodology or Clinical Evidence Base requires sign-off from ≥ 2 Clinical Advisory Board members.
- Whitepapers are public: hosted at `www.disciplineos.com/research`.
- Annual review takes place each Q1; all whitepapers either confirmed or revised.
- Whitepapers are versioned; historical versions remain resolvable.

## What whitepapers are not

- Not marketing collateral. Tone is academic-adjacent and careful.
- Not product changelogs. Methodology changes when the underlying approach changes, not when a button color moves.
- Not a substitute for the internal Technicals docs. These are the public-facing version; the Technicals docs (`../Technicals/`) are the engineering source of truth.
