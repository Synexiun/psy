## Core Principles (Read First)

1. **Intervention-first, not tracker-first.** We are the thing that happens *at the moment of urge*, not a log reviewed later.
2. **Cross-vertical by design.** Cravings, panic, procrastination, emotional reactivity, productivity lapses — all share substrate. Compete against 8 vertical apps with 1 integrated OS.
3. **Relapse is a data event, not a failure state.** The single most load-bearing UX decision in the product.
4. **Privacy as a moat, not a cost.** Edge ML, E2E for sensitive data, zero data licensing.
5. **Reject category anti-patterns.** No streak-reset shame. No leaderboards. No gamified recovery days. No paywalled crisis tools.
